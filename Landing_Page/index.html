My Personal Favourite

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a brilliant billionaire inventor and industrialist. He created a powered suit of armor to fight crime and protect the world.

The Hulk, also known as Dr. Bruce Banner, is a fictional superhero created by Marvel Comics. When Banner becomes angry or stressed, he transforms into the Hulk, a massive, green-skinned behemoth with incredible strength.

Wolverine, also known as Logan, is a popular Marvel Comics character known for his adamantium-laced skeleton and retractable claws. His regenerative healing factor allows him to recover from virtually any wound, making him nearly immortal.

Thor, the Norse god of thunder, is a prominent Marvel Comics superhero wielding the enchanted hammer Mjolnir. As the prince of Asgard, Thor possesses immense strength and the ability to control lightning.
